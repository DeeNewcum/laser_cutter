1. get OpenSCAD files from pmoes@thingiverse [2]
    • Platonic solids
    • Archimedean solids
    • multicolor Archimedean solids
    • Johnson solids [key]
    • Catalan solids
  2. text-edit the OpenSCAD file, make it solid, not hollow
    • in the "difference" function, comment out the last pair of lines
  3. open in OpenSCAD; Compile and Render; Export as STL
  4. open in Pepakura; Unfold; tweak; Export as Vector > EPS
    • 2D > Change Scale > Scale Factor ⇒ 1 (autoscale makes the polyhedra different sizes!)
    • when exporting as EPS, fill the faces, don't fill the flaps
  5. open in Inkscape; Extensions > Modify Path > Convert to Dashes; linewidth ⇒ 0.001 inch; Save a Copy > PDF
    • Path > Combine the folds (this is helpful); Combine the non-folds; Combine the filled-faces
    • final steps:
      • select all; fill⇒none
      • select folds; ungroup,ungroup,ungroup,ungroup; width⇒0.2px; dashes⇒widest; Extensions > Modify Path > Convert to Dashes
      • select all; linewidth⇒0.001 inch
  6. open in Acrobat Reader; print to the Epilog laser cutter
    • vector only; power=100%; speed=100%; frequency=500Hz
